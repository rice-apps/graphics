Please indicate on the application which positions you are interested in. Team leads and designers will be added to the end of the waitlist for their respective positions, but developers will be added to the end of the waitlist for specific projects. Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. Your position on the waitlist is determined solely by the timestamp of your application submission. Note that team leads that get off the waitlist will require an interview.

There are ways to move up the waitlist. For team leads, please directly reach out to Xilin Liu (xal1@rice.edu) to get an early interview. For designers, submit examples of your visual/web designs to xal1@rice.edu.

For developers, you may complete one of the listed mini-tasks for whichever project you are interested in. You must fork the project repository at www.github.com/rice-apps and then submit a pull request with your completed work. The team lead will review your submission and may offer you a position on the team. If you are declined, you will still be bumped to the top of the waitlist for that project.
