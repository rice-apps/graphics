Rice Elections

Click here to visit the Rice Elections app

Rice Elections is a simple, secure, and transparent way for student organizations to hold online elections. Conduct a secure election for your organization by providing the Rice NetIDs of the people who you wish to be eligible to vote. All voters are authenticated through Rice's NetID authentication system, and all ballots cast by voters are strictly anonymous! We do not tie your vote choices with your identity. In addition, the voters of an election can see the results after a specified delay. So the election is transparent! If you are interested in holding elections for your organization through Owlection, contact us.

Atlas

Click here to visit the Atlas app

The Rice Atlas app is an interactive and user-freindly way to locate any building on Rice's campus. Need help finding your where your class is held? Simply type the name of the building you wish to find into the Atlas search bar, and you will be shown it's exact location via Google Maps. Looking for more information about a specific place on campus? The search function also provides a link that you can follow to learn more about your destination, including hours of operation, contact info, and more. In addition, the Atlas app allows you to see the current location of all of the Rice shuttles on and around campus. This app is perfect for both Rice students and visitors to Rice University!

Servery App

Click here to visit the Servery App

The Servery App provides the menus for all of Rice University's dining serveries in one easy-to-read location. The Servery App allows you to compare what's being served at each servery so that you, the student, can find what best suits your appetite. The app is stocked with information on current and future meals so that you can plan your day accordingly. Users with dining restrictions can utilize the vegetarian and/or gluten-free only functions to view what options that fit their needs are available at each servery as well. Looking for information on when each servery is open? The Servery App also provides the hours of operation for each servery, so that you always know where and when to find the best food!

Schedule Planner

Click here to visit the Schedule Planner app

The Schedule Planner app allows students of Rice University to easily plan their class schedules via a clean and intuitive user interface. Students can use the app's search bar to access all classes in the Rice University course catalog, and then simply drag-and-drop their desired courses onto a visual display of their class schedule. Students can also take advantage of Schedule Planner's full multi-year degree planning, which includes validation to ensure major requirements and course pre-requisites are all being met. Additional features include searching for courses based on Distribution credit, hide/display course information, and more.

Wellbeing App

Click here to visit the Wellbeing App

The Wellbeing app is an initiative brought forth by Dean of Undergraduates John Hutchinson and the Rice University Student Association that provides a "hub for student wellbeing". The purpose of the Wellbeing app is to connect the undergraduate population at Rice University with all of the wellbeing resources offered on campus. Numerous features include a list of contact numbers and a quiz that points students to the correct resource for their specific need. In addition, the Wellbeing app includes an Uber-esque feature for night escort, an RUPD mobile blue button for emergency situations, and an alcohol estimator for the saftey of students. This app is the ultimate resource for the wellbeing and safety of the undergraduate student population at Rice University.

Petition App

Click here to visit the Petition App

The Petition App provides Rice students with a simple and secure platform to let their voices be heard. Students can use the Petition App to create, view, and sign petitions that will affect all aspects of life at Rice University. This app is ideal for use by the Student Association, within residential colleges, and more. The Petition App streamlines the voice of the student body to those in charge via a simple and user-friendly system. Start a petition today and let your voice be heard.
